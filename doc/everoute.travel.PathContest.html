Class: PathContest

everoute.travel. PathContest

new PathContest(rule)

A contest to provide the best paths to a given destination. A path may enter (and stay in) the contest as long as: Its previous steps are still the current best for their respective destination, No other contestant exists for the destination, Its cost is better than the previous destination (if different)
